Top TV Shows reviewed by Robert

My top 3 tv shows

Better Call Saul

Better Call Saul is a spin-off from the popular TV-show Breaking Bad created by Vince Gilligan.
It has a slow start but it takes up really fast and it's just a fanstastic tv show.

The Sopranos

New Jersey mob boss Tony Soprano deals with personal and professional issues in his home and business life that affect his mental state, leading him to seek professional psychiatric counseling.

The Wire

The Baltimore drug scene, as seen through the eyes of drug dealers and law enforcement.
